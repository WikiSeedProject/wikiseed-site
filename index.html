Wikiseed

Wikiseed is a project with the goal of preserving the entirety of Wikimedia. While many current preservation projects focus on the English Wikipedia, Wikiseed takes a project and language-agnostic approach, aiming to preserve all Wikimedia projects and languages for future generations. Wikiseed mirrors Wikimedia-provided database dumps via torrents and the Internet Archive.
